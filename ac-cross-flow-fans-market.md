<p><ul><li>Single Shaft</li><li>Double Shaft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>AC cross flow fans are categorized into two main types based on their shaft configuration: single shaft and double shaft. Single shaft fans feature one motor driving the impeller, resulting in a compact design and lower cost, ideal for simpler applications. In contrast, double shaft fans utilize two motors, providing enhanced airflow and efficiency, which is beneficial for larger setups requiring higher performance and redundancy. Both types are utilized in various industrial and commercial applications, offering tailored solutions for specific airflow needs.</p></p>
