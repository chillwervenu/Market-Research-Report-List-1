<p><ul><li>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) Sensor</li><li>Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acoustic Wave Technology Sensors are categorized into Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) and Bulk Acoustic Wave (BAW) sensors. SAW sensors utilize surface waves for detecting changes in temperature, pressure, and chemical composition, making them ideal for wireless and compact applications in various industries. BAW sensors, on the other hand, operate in the bulk of the material and are known for higher frequency responses and sensitivity, suitable for precise measurements in harsher environments. Both types contribute significantly to advancements in sensing technologies.</p></p>
