<p><strong>The Air Conditioning System Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Scroll Type Compressor</li><li>Rotary Type Compressor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air conditioning system compressor market features primarily two types: scroll type compressors and rotary type compressors. Scroll type compressors utilize two spiral-shaped scrolls to compress refrigerant, offering high efficiency and quiet operation, making them popular in residential and commercial applications. In contrast, rotary type compressors employ a rotating mechanism to compress the refrigerant, known for their compact design and reliability, often found in smaller air conditioning systems. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing cooling efficiency and system performance.</p></p>
