<p><strong>The Carbon Fibre Tape Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prepreg Tape</li><li>Dry Tape</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carbon fiber tape market comprises primarily two types: prepreg tape and dry tape. Prepreg tape is pre-impregnated with resin, offering enhanced strength and consistency, ideal for high-performance applications like aerospace and automotive industries. In contrast, dry tape is not treated with resin, allowing for flexibility in application methods, as users can control the amount and type of resin used during the manufacturing process. This versatility makes dry tape suitable for various applications where customization is needed.</p></p>
