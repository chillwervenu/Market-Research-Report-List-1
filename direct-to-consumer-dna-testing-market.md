<p><p>The Direct-to-Consumer DNA Testing market includes various types that cater to specific needs. Diagnostic screening assesses genetic predispositions to diseases, while prenatal testing analyzes a fetus's genetic health during pregnancy. Newborn screening identifies genetic conditions at birth, allowing for early intervention. Pre-implantation diagnosis evaluates embryos for genetic disorders before implantation during in vitro fertilization. Additionally, relationship testing verifies biological relationships, such as paternity or ancestry, providing insights into familial connections without requiring clinical intervention.</p></p>
