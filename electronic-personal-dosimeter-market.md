<p><p>The Electronic Personal Dosimeter market comprises several types, including PIN dosimeters, MOSFET dosimeters, and others. PIN dosimeters utilize semiconductor technology to measure radiation exposure with high sensitivity and real-time monitoring capabilities. MOSFET dosimeters leverage metal-oxide-semiconductor field-effect transistor technology for precise dose measurement and are known for their miniaturization and ease of use. Other dosimeter types may include thermoluminescent and optically stimulated luminescent dosimeters, catering to various radiation monitoring needs across different industries.</p></p>
