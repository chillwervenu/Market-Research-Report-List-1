<p><p>Epoxy composites are categorized primarily into three market types: glass, carbon, and others. Glass epoxy composites utilize glass fibers to enhance strength and durability, making them ideal for applications in construction and automotive sectors. Carbon epoxy composites employ carbon fibers, offering higher strength-to-weight ratios, commonly used in aerospace and high-performance sports equipment. Other types include aramid and natural fibers, which cater to specific requirements like flexibility or eco-friendliness, expanding the versatility and application range of epoxy composites across various industries.</p></p>
