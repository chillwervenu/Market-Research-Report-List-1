<p><ul><li>Static Frequency Converter</li><li>Rotary Frequency Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The frequency converter market is primarily categorized into static and rotary frequency converters. Static frequency converters utilize electronic components, such as inverters and rectifiers, to convert power frequencies efficiently and are commonly used in applications requiring compact solutions. In contrast, rotary frequency converters employ rotating machinery and electromagnetic principles to achieve frequency conversion, making them suitable for high-power applications and environments with robust demands. Both types serve diverse industries, including aerospace, mining, and manufacturing, addressing specific operational needs.</p></p>
