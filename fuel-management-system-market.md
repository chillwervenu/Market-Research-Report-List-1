<p><p>The Fuel Management System market is primarily categorized into card-based and on-site systems. Card-based systems utilize fuel cards for transactions, enabling seamless tracking and control of fuel purchases at designated stations. This method enhances convenience and accountability for fleet operators. On-site systems, in contrast, involve the installation of fuel management equipment at specific locations, allowing organizations to manage their fuel supply and consumption directly. These systems offer real-time monitoring and improved efficiency in fuel usage, catering to various operational needs.</p></p>
