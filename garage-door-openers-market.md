<p><p>The garage door openers market comprises various types, including belt drive, chain drive, and screw drive openers, along with others. Belt drive openers use a rubber belt for quieter operation and are ideal for homes with living spaces above. Chain drive openers are durable and cost-effective but can be noisier, making them suitable for detached garages. Screw drive openers rely on a threaded steel rod for movement, offering low maintenance. Other types may include smart openers and direct drive systems, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
