<p><p>The glaucoma treatment drugs market is divided into two main types: oral and injection-based therapies. Oral type glaucoma treatment drugs include medications taken by mouth that help reduce intraocular pressure through systemic absorption. These are often used for patients who prefer non-invasive options or have difficulty with eye drops. Injection type glaucoma treatment drugs involve administering medication directly into the eye, providing targeted relief and potentially longer-lasting effects. Both types aim to manage glaucoma effectively and prevent vision loss.</p></p>
