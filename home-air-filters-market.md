<p><p>The home air filters market includes several types designed to improve indoor air quality. HEPA air filters capture small particles like pollen and dust, while activated carbon filters absorb odors and volatile organic compounds. Ion and ozone air filters emit charged particles to trap pollutants, although ozone can be harmful in high concentrations. Electrostatic precipitator filters use electric charges to collect dust and allergens, making them effective for continuous use. Each type offers unique benefits for maintaining cleaner indoor air.</p></p>
