<p><ul><li>Portable meters</li><li>Fixed meters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Indoor Air Quality (IAQ) meters are classified into two main types: portable and fixed meters. Portable meters are handheld devices designed for quick, on-the-go assessment of air quality in various locations, making them ideal for fieldwork and immediate testing. In contrast, fixed meters are permanently installed in specific areas to continuously monitor air quality over time, providing ongoing data and alerts for long-term management. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring healthy indoor environments by detecting pollutants and optimizing ventilation.</p></p>
