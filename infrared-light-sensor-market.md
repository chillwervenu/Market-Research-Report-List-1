<p><p>The infrared light sensor market is segmented into two primary types: contact light sensors and non-contact light sensors. Contact light sensors require physical contact with the object being measured, making them suitable for applications like temperature sensing in various materials. Non-contact light sensors, on the other hand, detect infrared radiation without direct contact, allowing for remote monitoring of temperature and object presence. Each type serves unique applications across industries such as automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics, enhancing functionality and efficiency.</p></p>
