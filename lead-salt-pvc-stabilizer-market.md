<p><p>The Lead Salt PVC Stabilizer market is primarily divided into two types: Monomer Lead Salt Stabilizers and Composite Lead Salt Stabilizers. Monomer Lead Salt Stabilizers utilize individual lead compounds to enhance the thermal stability of PVC, ensuring longevity and durability.
