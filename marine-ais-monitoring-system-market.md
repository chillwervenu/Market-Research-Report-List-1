<p><p>The Marine AIS Monitoring System Market can be categorized into onshore-based and vessel-based systems. Onshore-based systems involve stationary installations at ports, coastal facilities, or monitoring centers, utilizing shore-based antennas to track multiple vessels' movements for maritime safety and traffic management. Conversely, vessel-based systems are integrated onboard ships, enabling real-time data transmission about the vessel's position, speed, and course directly from the ship. Both types enhance situational awareness and operational efficiency in maritime environments.</p></p>
