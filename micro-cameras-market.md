<p><p>The micro camera market is segmented into NIR (Near Infrared) and SWIR (Shortwave Infrared) cameras, each catering to specific applications. NIR cameras operate in the near-infrared spectrum, making them ideal for low-light conditions and various surveillance applications. In contrast, SWIR cameras function in the shortwave infrared range, allowing them to penetrate fog, smoke, and other obscurants, making them suitable for industrial, military, and agricultural uses. Together, they enhance imaging capabilities across diverse fields.</p></p>
