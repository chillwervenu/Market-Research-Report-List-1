<p><p>The Optical Palmprint Scanner market is divided into two main types: touchable and touchless scanners. Touchable scanners require direct contact with the scanning surface, allowing for high-resolution images of palm patterns. This method ensures accurate readings but may raise hygiene concerns. In contrast, touchless scanners capture palmprint images without physical contact, enhancing convenience and safety by minimizing the risk of cross-contamination. Both types serve various security and identification applications, each with distinct advantages addressing user preferences and safety requirements.</p></p>
