<p><strong>The Precision Cylindrical Grinders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Grinder</li><li>Plunge Grinders</li><li>End Face Grinders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precision cylindrical grinders are essential in the machining industry, classified mainly into three types. Longitudinal grinders operate by moving the workpiece past the grinding wheel along its length, suitable for long, cylindrical items. Plunge grinders are designed for removing material from a workpiece's diameter, using a vertical motion to achieve deeper cuts. End face grinders focus on flat surfaces, grinding the ends of cylindrical objects to ensure precision and finish, catering to various manufacturing needs in precision engineering.</p></p>
