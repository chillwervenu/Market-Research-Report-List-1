<p><ul><li>Embedded Steel Cylinder/Embedded Cylinder Pipe (ECP)</li><li>Outer Steel Cylinder/Lined Cylinder Pipe (LCP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Prestressed Concrete Cylinder Pipe (PCCP) market is primarily divided into two types: Embedded Steel Cylinder Pipes (ECP) and Outer Steel Cylinder/Lined Cylinder Pipes (LCP). ECP features a steel cylinder completely encased in concrete, providing high tensile strength and resistance against internal pressure. LCP, on the other hand, involves an outer steel liner that protects the concrete core while enhancing durability. Both types are crucial for various applications, including water transportation and sewage systems, ensuring reliability and efficiency.</p></p>
