<p><p>The property management software market can be categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based solutions. On-premise software is installed directly on a property manager's local infrastructure, offering greater control over data and customization but requiring higher upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based software operates over the internet, providing flexibility, ease of access, and lower initial expenses. This model allows for automatic updates and scalability, making it increasingly popular among property management professionals.</p></p>
