<p><p>The radiation detection products market is divided into two main types: dose detection and non-dose detection. Dose detection focuses on measuring the amount of radiation absorbed by an individual, providing critical data for health and safety assessments. Non-dose detection, on the other hand, includes equipment that identifies the presence of radiation without quantifying exposure levels, typically used for safety monitoring in various environments. Together, these products enhance safety, security, and regulatory compliance in industries like healthcare, nuclear energy, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
