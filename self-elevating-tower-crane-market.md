<p><p>The self-elevating tower crane market is divided into two main types: interior and external. Interior self-elevating tower cranes are designed for use within buildings, allowing for efficient vertical lifting in confined spaces, often during construction or renovation projects. External self-elevating tower cranes, on the other hand, are positioned outside structures, providing greater reach and capacity for lifting heavy materials to higher elevations, making them ideal for large-scale construction sites. Both types enhance productivity and safety in their respective environments.</p></p>
