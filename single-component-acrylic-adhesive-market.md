Solvent-based adhesives contain organic solvents, providing strong bonding capabilities and resistance to environmental factors but may emit volatile organic compounds. Other types include specialty formulations designed for specific applications, such as heat- or temperature-resistant adhesives, catering to various industrial and consumer needs.</p></p>
