<p><p>The smart catheters market is categorized into single lumen and multi lumen types. Single lumen catheters feature a single channel for fluid or medication delivery, making them suitable for straightforward procedures. In contrast, multi lumen catheters have multiple channels, allowing for simultaneous administration of different fluids or therapies, which enhances versatility and efficiency in treatment. The choice between these types depends on clinical needs, complexity of the procedure, and patient-specific factors, influencing their application in healthcare settings.</p></p>
