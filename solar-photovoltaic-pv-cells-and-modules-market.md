<p><p>Solar photovoltaic (PV) cells and modules utilize various technologies to enhance efficiency and performance. Passivated Emitter Rear Cell (PERC) technology improves light capture and reduces recombination losses. Tunnel Oxide Passivated Contact (TOPCon) offers high efficiency with advanced passivation techniques. Heterojunction Technology (HJT) combines crystalline silicon with thin-film layers, optimizing performance. Heterojunction Interdigitated Back Contact (IBC) features back-side contacts for reduced shading and enhanced efficiency. Other market types include emerging technologies and innovations that continue to drive the solar energy sector forward.</p></p>
