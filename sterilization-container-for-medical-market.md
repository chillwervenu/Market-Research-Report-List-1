<p><p>Sterilization containers for the medical market are essential for ensuring the safe and efficient sterilization of medical instruments. There are two main types: filter type and valve type. Filter type containers utilize filters to allow gases to circulate while preventing contaminants, ensuring thorough sterilization. In contrast, valve type containers feature built-in valves that regulate airflow, facilitating the escape of steam or gases while maintaining a sterile environment. Both types are crucial for enhancing patient safety and instrumentation longevity in healthcare settings.</p></p>
