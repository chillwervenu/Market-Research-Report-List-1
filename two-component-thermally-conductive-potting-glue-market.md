<p><p>The Two-Component Thermally Conductive Potting Glue market is divided into two main types: Additive Type and Condensation Type. Additive Type involves the use of thermally conductive fillers mixed with a resin and hardener to enhance performance without changing the base polymer properties. In contrast, Condensation Type cures through a chemical reaction that releases small molecules, often resulting in improved adhesion and lower shrinkage. Both types are crucial for applications requiring effective heat dissipation and electrical insulation in electronic devices.</p></p>
