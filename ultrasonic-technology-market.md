<p><strong>The Ultrasonic Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Detection Ultrasound</li><li>Power Ultrasound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasonic technology market can be divided into two main types: detection ultrasound and power ultrasound. Detection ultrasound focuses on identifying and measuring various parameters, such as distance or flaws in materials, using high-frequency sound waves. It's commonly used in applications like medical imaging and non-destructive testing. Power ultrasound, on the other hand, utilizes high-intensity ultrasound waves to perform tasks such as cleaning, emulsifying, or accelerating chemical reactions, often seen in industrial and food processing applications.</p></p>
