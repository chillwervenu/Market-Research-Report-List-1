<p><p>Wireless medical alert systems utilize various communication technologies to ensure patient safety and timely assistance. Zigbee offers low-power, short-range connections ideal for home environments. Wi-Fi facilitates real-time monitoring and alerts through internet connectivity, while Bluetooth provides personal device integration for immediate response. Cellular systems enable wider coverage and reliable communication, especially in emergencies when Wi-Fi or Bluetooth is unavailable. Other technologies may include NFC and proprietary systems, each offering unique advantages based on user needs and environments.</p></p>
