<p><strong>The Zircon Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magmatic Zircon</li><li>Hydrothermal Zircon</li><li>Metamorphic Zircon</li><li>Detrital Zircon</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The zircon market is segmented into four main types: Magmatic zircon, formed from crystallized magma, is primarily found in igneous rocks and is popular in industrial applications. Hydrothermal zircon originates from fluid-rich environments and is often associated with mineral deposits. Metamorphic zircon is produced under high pressure and temperature conditions, commonly in metamorphic rocks, and is valued for geological studies. Detrital zircon, eroded from its source, is used in sedimentological research and age-dating of rocks, reflecting a diverse range of applications.</p></p>
